Main Theme of the 
Fantaisie-Impromptu
Frederic Chopin's 
Fantaisie-Impromptu in C# minor Op. 
posth. 66 is a solo piano composition. 
It was composed in 1834 and published 
posthumously in 1855 even though Chopin 
had instructed that none of his 
unpublished manuscripts be published. 
The Fantaisie-Impromptu is one of 
Chopin's most frequently performed and 
popular compositions.
